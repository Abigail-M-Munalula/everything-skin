Meet Abigail Munalula, a certified Skin Health Expert & Specialist who has spent over four years helping people stop guessing and start understanding their skin better by offering expert advice on products that actually work for each skin type.

Frustrated by the beauty industry's habit of pushing beauty trends and skin care products before education—leading to adverse effects that are damaging to the natural skin barrier—Abigail built this website as a judgment-free classroom.

Her goal is to educate people not only about skin beauty but also focus on promoting healthy beauty practices by offering the right recommendations, ensuring that your approach to barrier maintenance is structurally sound and data-driven.
