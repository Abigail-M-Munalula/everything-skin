1.0 Skin Types

Oily skin

Characterized by overactive sebaceous glands producing excess lipids. Often presents visible pores and a reflective surface.

  • Prone to acne and pore blockages.
  • Requires targeted sebum regulation.
  • Resilient, signs of aging develop slower.

Dry skin

Lacks the natural oil production needed to sustain a healthy moisture barrier. Often feels tight and may show flaky texture.

  • High rate of trans-epidermal water loss.
  • Saves cell integrity via external ceramide lipids.
  • Prone to fine premature lines.

Sensitive skin

Features an easily compromised stratum corneum layer allowing external irritants to trigger inflammatory pathways readily.

  • Exhibits temporary redness or burning.
  • Demands fragrance-free formulations.
  • Focuses purely on deep barrier support.

2.0 Ingredients guide

Ceramides
Waxy lipid molecules that act as the "mortar" between skin cells, essential for repairing structural barrier integrity.
BEST FOR: DRY / SENSITIVE
USE: PM CYCLE
Salicylic Acid
An oil-soluble Beta Hydroxy Acid (BHA) that penetrates deep into cellular walls to clear trapped lipids and debris.
BEST FOR: OILY / CONGESTED
USE: AM OR PM
Niacinamide
Vitamin B3 profile. Regulates excessive sebum excretion, soothes micro-redness, and works on structural visibility of pores.
BEST FOR: ALL SKIN TYPES
USE: AM & PM
